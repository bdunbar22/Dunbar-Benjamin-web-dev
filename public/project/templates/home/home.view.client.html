Freedom to Flip

Welcome to Freedom to Flip!

This project will be an art sharing and critic platform for human movement. It will allow for the upload of images, videos and text posts so that members can share their accomplishments. The app will also allow for fun competitions, were users submit a photo or video to a competition that another user has created. A judge or judge panel will then vote for a winning entry. A user's profile will include how many fun competitions they have won. It will also show the posts they have made. Finally, in order to allow for real world collaboration, users can create and join events that will happen in the real world.

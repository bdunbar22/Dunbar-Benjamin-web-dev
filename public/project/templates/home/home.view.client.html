Freedom to Flip

Welcome to Freedom to Flip!

This project will be an art sharing and critic platform for human movement. It will allow for the upload of images, videos and text posts so that members can share their accomplishments. The app will also allow for fun competitions, were users submit a photo or video to a competition that another user has created. The competition can then be voted on for a winning video and part of a users profile will be how many fun competitions they have won. Voting will be encouraged via limits on the number of videos you can submit based on number of competitions you have voted in.
